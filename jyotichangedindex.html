How to find Area of Rectangle and Triangle

To find the Area of different Quadrilaterls we need to find the Area of the Rectangle and Triangle. For this, we need a to do some experiment.

Things needed

  • A graph paper of each cube is 1cm
  • A pencil to draw
  1. First draw the Rectangle on the grid paper.
  2. Count the square inside the rectangle

Now , we need to find Area of the Triangle using the formula of the Area of the Rectangle.
As we know area of the Rectangle is A = L * w where L = length and W= width of the rectangle.
We need to cut the Rectangle into half from the middle as shown in the video.
Triangle is 1/2 *B *H where h= height of the triangle and b= base of the triangle.
